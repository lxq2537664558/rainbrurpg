7 Reporting Bugs

If you find a bug in RainbruRPG, please send electronic mail to rainbru@free.fr. Include the version number, which you can find by running `RainbruRPG --version'. Also include in your message the output that the program produced and the output you expected.

If you have other questions, comments or suggestions about RainbruRPG, contact the author via electronic mail to rainbru@free.fr. The author will try to help you out, although he may not have time to fix your problems.
